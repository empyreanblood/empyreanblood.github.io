JcJenson, ZNA Systems, and Bliss.

Before the great calamity, there were three main producers of drones. These companies competed in the creation and distrubution of these drones, it seemed almost every person had at least 2 drones in their household. While they all form the same foundation, these companies specialized in their own fields. JcJenson, specialized in terraforming planets and held drones responsible for labor. ZNA Systems, performing military roles and filling in for the lives lost in previous wars. Bliss, for the comfort of humanity, specializing in societal roles.
